All about chickens

A chicken is a bird, it has two wings, two legs and lays eggs.
Many people raise chickens for their eggs and to eventually eat them.
A male chicken is called a rooster and a female chicken is called a hen.

Famous Chickens

  • Chicken Little
  • Big Red Chicken from Dora the Explorer
  • Foghorn Leghorn from Bugs Bunny

There are many different breeds of chickens.
In Canada, we have developed two special breeds of chicken that can survive in our cold climate. They are

  • Chantecler Chicken
  • Red Shaver Chicken
